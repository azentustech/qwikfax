What is QwikFax

QwikFax is an app developed to allow you to send fax to any number in the world, in 3 simple steps.

What is credit?

Credit allow you to send fax when using this app. 1 credit = 1 page. If your document is 5 pages, you need to have minimum 5 credits to fax your document.

Does the credit expire?

No. Credit will not expire, or transferable or refundable.

How do I know if the fax is delivered?

You will receive notification on your phone once the delivery is completed. You will also receive the fax delivery status in your email.

What happen when the fax failed to deliver?

We do not charge for fail deliveries. Your credit will be reflected and you can resend the document if you wish.
